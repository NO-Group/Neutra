About NEUTRA

Professional music discovery, reimagined

What is NEUTRA?

NEUTRA is a professional music discovery platform designed for clarity and focus. We aggregate music metadata and provide direct links to official streaming platforms where you can listen to and support artists.

Our platform does not host music files for download. We believe in connecting listeners with artists through legitimate channels.

How It Works

  • Discovery: Browse our curated catalog of tracks and albums
  • Information: View detailed metadata including artist, album, and duration
  • Redirection: Click through to your preferred streaming platform
  • UZT Exclusive: Selected tracks may include preview playback

Terms of Use

By using NEUTRA, you agree to the following:

  • This platform is for music discovery purposes only
  • We do not provide music downloads

  • We are not responsible for content on external sites

Privacy

NEUTRA respects your privacy:

  • We do not collect personal data
  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not require user accounts
  • All browsing is anonymous

UZT Platform

UZT is our exclusive content section. It is entirely controlled by the site owner and may feature:

  • Full track streaming (owner-enabled only)
  • Preview clips
  • Exclusive releases

UZT content cannot be uploaded by users. All content is curated and managed through our internal catalog system.

Contact

For inquiries regarding content, corrections, or partnerships, please reach out through official channels.
